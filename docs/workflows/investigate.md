# Investigate (Cases)

**Pages**: 
*   `Case Board` (`/cases`)
*   `Threat Intel` (`/threat-intel`)

## Case Management
The **Case Board** uses a Kanban layout to track the status of security incidents.

### Case Stages
1.  **Open**: New cases waiting to be assigned.
2.  **In Progress**: An analyst is actively investigating.
3.  **Resolved**: The incident has been contained and closed.

## Investigation Steps
1.  Click on a Case Card to open the **Case Details** view.
2.  **Timeline**: Review the sequence of events that triggered the alerts.
3.  **Observables**: Check the "Observables" tab for suspicious IPs, Hashes, or Domains.
4.  **Threat Intelligence**: Use the global search or navigate to **Threat Intel** to check the reputation of these observables against known threat databases.
