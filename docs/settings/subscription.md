# Subscription & Billing

**Page**: `Settings > Subscription` (`/settings/subscription`)

Manage your zcrAI subscription, view usage limits, and upgrade your plan.

## Plan Details
The dashboard displays your current tier:
*   **Free**: Basic protection for small teams.
*   **Pro**: Advanced features and longer retention.
*   **Enterprise**: Custom limits and dedicated support.

## Usage Metrics
Monitor your consumption against plan limits:
*   **Users**: Number of active user accounts.
*   **Retention**: How many days data is stored (e.g., 7 days vs 30 days).
*   **Data Volume**: Total data ingested/stored (in GB).

## Billing Status
*   **Active**: Subscription is in good standing.
*   **Past Due**: There was a payment issue; please update payment method to avoid service interruption.
